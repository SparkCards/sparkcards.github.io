Frequently Asked Questions


Creating a Deck
  1. Tap the button at the top right of the decks screen.
  2. Choose the "Create" option.
  3. You can enter the deck title in the space provided. Decks will save automatially.
  4. Use the folder button to manage folders. For more information on folders, see the section "Managing Folders."
  5. To add a card, tap the button. For more information on creating cards, see the section "Creating a Card."
  6. To delete the deck, tap the trash button at the top left of the screen.

Creating a Card
  1. Navigate to to the deck you want to add cards to. If need to create a deck, see the section "Creating a Deck."
  2. Tap the button.
  3. You can choose text or image using the controls for the front and back. In the image tab, you can choose to upload an image or create a drawing.
  4. Enter either text or an image/drawing for the front and back. You can choose a card theme using the controls at the bottom of the page.
  5. Tap the "Create" button at the top right of the screen to create the card. You can tap the card in the list to edit it.

Exporting a Deck
  1. Tap the deck you want to export.
  2. Tap the share button on the top left of the page.
  3. You can choose to export as either text or Qr Codes:
    • Qr Codes - Your deck will be exported in a series of QR Codes. You can share or print the QR Codes. Anyone with the SaprkCards app on their device can scan the QR Codes using the app to get the deck.
    • Text - Your deck will be exported in a string of numbers and letters. You can share this text any way you want.
  4. For more information on importing a deck, see the section "Importing a Deck."

Importing a Deck
  1. Tap the plus (+) button at the top right of the decks screen.
  2. Choose the "Import" option.
  3. You can choose to enter text or scan QR Codes.
    • Text - choose this option if you want to import a deck from text. Paste or drag the text into the text field. The text is generated by exporting your deck as text.
    • Scan QR Code - choose this option if you want to scan deck QR Codes. These are created by exporting a deck as QR Codes. Point your device's camera at the first QR Code. It will scan automatically. Scan all QR Codes one at a time in any order.
  4. If another deck already exists with the same name, you will be prompted to merge the deck if you choose.
  5. For more information on exporting a deck, see the section "Exporting a Deck."

Managing Folders
Creating a Folder
  1. Tap the title at the top of the decks screen. It should read "All Decks" if you have no folders yet.
  2. Choose "New Folder"
  3. Type the name of the folder in the text field that appears.
  4. Tap Create.
Moving Decks
  1. Long press the deck you want to move
  2. Choose "Move To..."
    1. To move the deck to a folder, choose the folder you want to move the deck to.
    2. To remove the deck from a folder, choose the folder with the checkmark next to it to remove the deck from that folder.
Editing a Folder
  1. Tap the title at the top of the decks screen. It should read "All Decks" if you have no folder selected.
  2. Choose the folder you want to edit.
  3. Tap the title at the top of the decks screen, which should read the name of the folder you chose.
  4. Choose "Folder Options"
  5. Here you can choose to edit the folder's name of delete the folder. Deleting the folder does not delete the decks inside it.
