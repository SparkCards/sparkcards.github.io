SparkCards is a flashcard app built from the ground up to be powerful and effective yet simple and easy to use. The app automatically and intelligently chooses the cards for you to study by using learning methods based on research into efficient and long-term memorization concepts. You can create and study flashcards offline, anytime, anywhere.
