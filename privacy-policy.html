SparkCards App Privacy Policy


This application may personal information, including your name. This information never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone unless the iCloud Sync feature is enabled, in which case it will be stored in your (the user's) iCloud drive documents, handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of these changes by posting them here and by placing a prominent notice within the app. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at sparkcardshelp@gmail.com.

This policy is effective as of 2022/08/1.
